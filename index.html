Frequently Asked Questions

This section answers the most common questions I receive about my work. Whether you're looking for a website, video editing, or a standout resume — here's what you need to know before getting started.

1. What kind of websites do you design?

I create both basic and advanced websites tailored to your personal or business needs. From clean landing pages to multi-page sites with custom features, I focus on design, responsiveness, and user experience.

2. Do you offer responsive and mobile-friendly designs?

Yes! Every website I build is fully responsive — meaning it works smoothly on phones, tablets, and desktops to ensure the best viewing experience for your visitors.

3. What type of video editing services do you provide?

I handle both basic (cuts, subtitles, transitions) and advanced video edits (color grading, effects, motion graphics) for platforms like YouTube, Instagram, and TikTok — tailored to your content's goals.

Definitely. I design editable CVs/resumes (usually in Canva or Word) that you can easily update yourself in the future — no design experience needed.
